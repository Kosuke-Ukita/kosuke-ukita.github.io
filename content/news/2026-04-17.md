### Selected for International Travel Grant — Telecommunications Advancement Foundation

<p class="article-date">2026-04-17</p>

I have been selected as a recipient of the **[International Travel Grant (April 2026)](https://www.taf.or.jp/grant-b/02/)** awarded by the [Telecommunications Advancement Foundation](https://www.taf.or.jp/).

<div class="section-title">about the grant</div>

The Telecommunications Advancement Foundation provides international travel grants to support researchers in science and technology fields attending overseas academic conferences. This grant covers travel and accommodation expenses for presenting research abroad, contributing to international academic exchange.

This grant supports attendance at **AISTATS 2026** (Tangier, Morocco), where the paper *"High-Performance Self-Supervised Learning by Joint Training of Flow Matching"* was presented as a presentation.

- **Grant:** International Travel Grant (2026 April selection)
- **Amount:** JPY ¥290,000
- **Organization:** [The Telecommunications Advancement Foundation](https://www.taf.or.jp/)
- **Purpose:** Attending AISTATS 2026, Tangier, Morocco

### 海外渡航旅費援助（2026年度4月）に採択

<p class="article-date">2026-04-17</p>

[公益財団法人 電気通信普及財団](https://www.taf.or.jp/)の　**[海外渡航旅費援助（2026年度4月）](https://www.taf.or.jp/files/2270/85011411.pdf)**　に援助決定者として採択されました．

<div class="section-title">about the grant</div>

電気通信普及財団は，情報通信の普及・発展を目的とする公益財団法人です．科学技術分野の研究者が海外の学術会議で研究成果を発表するための旅費・宿泊費を支援し，国際的な学術交流の促進に貢献しています．

本助成は，**AISTATS 2026**（Tangier, Morocco）において論文 *"High-Performance Self-Supervised Learning by Joint Training of Flow Matching"* を発表するための渡航費を支援するものです．

- **助成：** 海外渡航旅費援助（2026年度4月採択）
- **助成額：** JPY ¥290,000
- **助成機関：** [公益財団法人 電気通信普及財団](https://www.taf.or.jp/)
- **用途：** AISTATS 2026 (Tangier, Morocco) への参加・発表
