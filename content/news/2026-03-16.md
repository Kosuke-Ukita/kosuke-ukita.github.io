### Received Student Travel Award at PAKDD 2026

<p class="article-date">2026-03-16</p>

I received the **[Student Travel Award](https://www.pakdd2026.org/awardees#:~:text=Kosuke%20Ukita%20(Kyushu%20Institue%20of%20Technology))** at [PAKDD 2026](https://www.pakdd2026.org/), awarded to outstanding student presenters at the conference.

<div class="section-title">about the award</div>

The PAKDD Student Travel Award is granted to doctoral students presenting high-quality research at the conference. This award was received in recognition of the Oral presentation of **"Efficient Inference for Flow Matching via Unified Path CFG"** and covers part of the travel and accommodation expenses for attending the conference in Hong Kong.

- **Award:** Student Travel Award (USD $500)
- **Awarded by:** The 30th Pacific-Asia Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining (PAKDD 2026)
- **Related paper:** [Efficient Inference for Flow Matching via Unified Path CFG](/assets/papers/ukita2026efficient.pdf)

### 受賞：Student Travel Award · PAKDD 2026

<p class="article-date">2026-03-16</p>

[PAKDD 2026](https://www.pakdd2026.org/)において**[Student Travel Award](https://www.pakdd2026.org/awardees#:~:text=Kosuke%20Ukita%20(Kyushu%20Institue%20of%20Technology))**を受賞しました．

<div class="section-title">about the award</div>

PAKDD Student Travel Awardは，優秀な研究発表を行った学生発表者に授与される賞です．**"Efficient Inference for Flow Matching via Unified Path CFG"** のOral発表やこれまでの実績が評価され，香港での国際会議参加に関する旅費の一部が支援されました．PAKDD会議の学生サポーターとしても活動を経験でき，受付やモデレータなどサポーターを務めます．

- **受賞：** Student Travel Award（USD $500相当）
- **授与機関：** The 30th Pacific-Asia Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining (PAKDD 2026)
- **関連論文：** [Efficient Inference for Flow Matching via Unified Path CFG](/assets/papers/ukita2026efficient.pdf)
