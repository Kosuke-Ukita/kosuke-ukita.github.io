## Paper accepted as Spotlight at AISTATS 2026

:date-tag{:date="date"}

Our paper **"High-Performance Self-Supervised Learning by Joint Training of Flow Matching"** was accepted as a **Spotlight presentation (Top 3%)** at [AISTATS 2026](https://virtual.aistats.org/).

### About the paper

This work proposes a novel self-supervised learning framework for sensor data by jointly training a Flow Matching generative model with a representation learning objective.
By leveraging the continuous normalizing flow structure, we demonstrate that the learned representations achieve strong performance on downstream tasks while significantly reducing computational cost compared to diffusion-based approaches.

- **Conference:** The 29th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Statistics (AISTATS 2026)
- **Presentation type:** Spotlight (Top 3% of submissions)
- **Location:** Tangier, Morocco — May 2026
