### Paper accepted as Oral at PAKDD 2026

<p class="article-date">2026-02-08</p>

> **Efficient Inference for Flow Matching via Unified Path CFG**<br>
> Kosuke Ukita, Tsuyoshi Okita

Our paper was accepted as an **Oral presentation** at [PAKDD 2026](https://www.pakdd2026.org/), the 30th Pacific-Asia Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining.

<div class="section-title">about the paper</div>

Flow Matching with Classifier-Free Guidance (CFG) typically requires two forward passes per inference step — one conditional and one unconditional — making deployment costly. This paper proposes **Unified Path CFG**, a method that consolidates the conditional and unconditional paths into a single trajectory. The unified path maintains guidance quality while **halving the number of function evaluations**, achieving approximately 2× speedup at inference time. Experiments on wearable sensor data for activity recognition confirm that the approach matches or surpasses standard CFG performance at a fraction of the cost.

- **Conference:** The 30th Pacific-Asia Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining (PAKDD 2026)
- **Presentation type:** Oral
- **Location:** Hong Kong, China — June 2026

### 論文採択：PAKDD 2026 · Oral

<p class="article-date">2026-02-08</p>

> **Efficient Inference for Flow Matching via Unified Path CFG**<br>
> Kosuke Ukita, Tsuyoshi Okita

第30回 Pacific-Asia Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining（[PAKDD 2026](https://www.pakdd2026.org/)）にてOral presentationとして採択されました．

<div class="section-title">about the paper</div>

Flow Matchingにおける Classifier-Free Guidance（CFG）は，推論ステップごとに条件付き・無条件の2回フォワードパスを必要とするため，計算コストが大きいという課題があります．本論文では，条件付きパスと無条件パスを単一の軌跡に統合する **Unified Path CFG** を提案します．これにより，誘導品質を維持しながら**推論コストを約1/2に削減**します．ウェアラブルセンサデータを用いた人間行動認識タスクにおいて，標準的なCFGと同等以上の精度を大幅に低い計算コストで達成することを確認しました．

- **会議名：** The 30th Pacific-Asia Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining (PAKDD 2026)
- **発表形式：** 口頭発表
- **開催地：** Hong Kong, China - June 2026
